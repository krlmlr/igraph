# igraph_vs_seq() and 4 others

The deprecated `igraph_vs_seq()`, `igraph_vss_seq()`, `igraph_es_seq()`, `igraph_ess_range()`, and `igraph_vector_init_seq()` were removed. Use the `range` alternatives instead of the old `seq` ones.
