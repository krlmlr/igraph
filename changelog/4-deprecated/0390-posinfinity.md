# IGRAPH_POSINFINITY, IGRAPH_NEGINFINITY, IGRAPH_INFINITY

The macros `IGRAPH_POSINFINITY` and `IGRAPH_NEGINFINITY` were removed. Use `IGRAPH_INFINITY` and `-IGRAPH_INFINITY` instead.
