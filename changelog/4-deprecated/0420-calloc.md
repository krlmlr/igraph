# igraph_Calloc(), igraph_Realloc(), igraph_Free()

Removed `igraph_Calloc()`, `igraph_Realloc()` and `igraph_Free()`. Use `IGRAPH_CALLOC()`, `IGRAPH_REALLOC()` and `IGRAPH_FREE()` instead.
