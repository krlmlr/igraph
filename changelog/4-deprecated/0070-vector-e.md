# igraph_vector_e() and 3 others

The deprecated `igraph_vector_e()`, `igraph_vector_e_ptr()`, `igraph_matrix_e()` and `igraph_matrix_e_ptr()` were removed. Use the alternatives ending in `_get()` and `_get_ptr()` instead.

> The deprecated `igraph_vector_e()`, `igraph_vector_e_ptr()`, `igraph_matrix_e()` and `igraph_matrix_e_ptr()` were removed. Use the alternatives ending in `_get()` and `_get_ptr()` instead.
